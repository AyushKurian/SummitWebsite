CanaryHosting - Privacy Policy


The following contract constitutes the client’s rights while consuming or in relation
with CanaryHosting. The provider has vowed to follow and comply with the
General Data Privacy Regulation, Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act,
California Consumer Privacy Act and UK General Data Privacy Regulation.

The governing laws and forces of this document are the United Kingdom.

What are my rights?


(EU, UK) A customer of EU or UK residence is under protection by the GDPR and
or UK GDPR, which you may find more information on here:
[https://gdpr-info.eu/] Said act states in detail the following rights:

• Right to request all data we have on you
• Right to prohibit us from accessing your data
• Right to restrict data processing
• Right to prevent data being used commercially
• Right to retract your data*

* = depending on the location of the data, may different legal requirements be
enforced. Amounts of data such as billing information are required to be kept for 7
years by the UK HMRC and 2 years by US law.

(California, US) Clientele with current residence within in the state of California is
by all measures of online interactions protected by the Californian Consumer
Privacy Act, the provider declares to respect said act and therefore enforces the
following rights on the client:

• Prohibit the provider from selling your data
• Know how, what and where your data is stored
• Right to remove your data* (* = seen above)

I am under 13 years of age (COPPA):


As the US’ Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act requires, we, CanaryHosting
are prohibited from collecting data and engaging in business with, by said law
unless written consent is given from the underage individual’s parental guardians.

Applications for such consent approvals may be sent to
legal@hostingbycanary.com

CanaryHosting shall be in no way shape or form liable for underage users on its
platforms, said users are the responsibility of their guardians, yet conversely the
provider shall at all times look out for, diminish and remove any and all accounts
in usage by underage clientele.

Do we sell data?


CanaryHosting does use data commercially but in specific cases the provider may
have to share said data with 3rd parties due to legalities.

Said third parties may include government agencies and datacenter operators.

Furthermore all data is hosted by Brixly.uk in the United States of America where strict data
regulations apply, protecting your information.

What data types do we store?


By requirement of Her Majesty’s Revenue and Customs is CanaryHosting required to keep the following data:

• Invoices
• Account Information
• IP Addresses
• Transactions
